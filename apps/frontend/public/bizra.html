About Us

From sustainable farming to satellite-powered resilience

Bizra Farms Integrated Nigeria Limited is a leading agribusiness company that employs precision farming and smart agriculture to create sustainable value across the value chain. We specialize in rice farming, animal feed production, renewable agriculture, and semi-robotic farming. EconomicBridge extends that mission into AI and satellite intelligence — for aid delivery, climate adaptation, and rural economic opportunity.
