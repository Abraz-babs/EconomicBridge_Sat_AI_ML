About Us

From sustainable farming to satellite-powered resilience

Bizra Farms Integrated Nigeria Limited is an agribusiness company focused on precision farming, sustainable food systems, smart distribution, and technology-enabled agriculture. EconomicBridge extends that mission into AI and satellite intelligence for aid delivery, climate adaptation, and rural economic opportunity.

Precision Farming Data-guided crop decisions, field monitoring, and yield protection.
Satellite Intelligence Sentinel, FIRMS, VIIRS, World Bank, and open-data pipelines.
Aid Coordination Coverage gaps, duplication signals, and response planning.
Farmer Alerts SMS-ready warnings for conflict, flood, drought, and crop threats.
