# EconomicBridge — 2-Minute Pitch Video (v2)

More cinematic; reflects the now-live platform (trained AI, satellite NDVI,
full coverage, governments onboarding). No security-platform / Kebbi mention.
Voiceover ~300 words ≈ 2:00. Record VOICE first, cut footage to it.

## SEG 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:18)
SHOW: Founder in a real field at golden hour; sweeping wide shots of farmland
and a rural settlement; a farmer's face.
SAY: "Every year across West Africa, a farmer watches a whole season's work
vanish. To a flood no one warned them about. A disease they couldn't name. A
conflict that swept through overnight. Not because the danger was invisible —
but because no one was watching from above."

## SEG 2 — SOLUTION (0:18–0:38)
SHOW: Screen rec — Bizra front door → dashboard Overview: the map, pulsing
halos, the rotating intel ticker.
SAY: "I'm [name], founder of Bizra Farms. We built EconomicBridge — and now,
someone is watching. A satellite and AI platform, live today, monitoring more
than seven hundred local areas across Nigeria, Ghana and Senegal — every
single day, from space."

## SEG 3 — PROOF (0:38–1:18) — three quick scenes
1) CropGuard money shot: hold a diseased leaf to camera, then screen rec:
   upload it, the TRAINED badge, the diagnosis + Grad-CAM heatmap.
2) Satellite vegetation: the NDVI / crop-health view across a state (no photo).
3) Farmland halos pulsing, then ShockGuard flood/drought on the map.
SAY: "Photograph a sick crop, and our AI names the disease in seconds — and
shows you exactly where it looked. From orbit, we track the health of every
field, week by week. We see floods through the clouds, droughts before they
bite, and we predict farmer-herder conflict twenty-four to seventy-two hours
before it erupts."

## SEG 4 — INCLUSION (1:18–1:38)
SHOW: a farmer holding a basic feature phone showing an SMS; the Admin SMS
panel with English / French / Hausa previews.
SAY: "And the warning reaches the people who need it — not as an app they
can't download, but as a simple text message, in their own language. No
smartphone. No literacy barrier. No one left behind."

## SEG 5 — VISION + CTA (1:38–2:00)
SHOW: Overview map zooming out to the whole West Africa region; cut to founder
looking at camera; end card: logo + live URL + "Bizra Farms Integrated".
SAY: "EconomicBridge turns satellites into a shield for the people who feed our
continent. It is live, it is working, and governments are already coming on
board. We are built for fifty-two regions across West Africa. Help us make
every farmer visible, every harvest protected, and every life seen in time.
EconomicBridge. Planting the seeds of tomorrow."

## Production notes (same as v1)
VOICE first: quiet room, phone ~30cm, 3 takes/segment, ~10% slower than
natural, +10s room silence at the end. FIELD: landscape 1080p/4K, sun behind
camera, golden hour, hold each shot 8–10s still, 2–3 takes. SCREEN: full-screen
browser (F11), bookmarks hidden, slow deliberate mouse (pause 2s on readables),
one clip per scene; CropGuard upload→TRAINED→Grad-CAM in one take on real
Sentinel data. HAND-OFF to the editor: give this script as the edit map; ask
for light hopeful African instrumental (low), SUBTITLES (judges often watch
muted), and the end card with the URL.
