# EconomicBridge — 2-Minute Submission Video: Script + Shot Map

Voiceover ~290 words ≈ 1:55. Record VOICE first, match footage to it.

## SEG 1 — Problem (0:00–0:20)
SHOW: Founder on camera in a real field (golden hour); wide farmland shots.
SAY: "I'm [name], founder of Bizra Farms in Kebbi State, Nigeria. Across West
Africa, smallholder farmers lose their crops, their land, and sometimes their
lives — to conflicts, floods, droughts and disease they never saw coming.
Governments and aid agencies want to help, but they're making decisions blind."

## SEG 2 — Solution (0:20–0:40)
SHOW: Screen rec — Bizra front door → Enter EconomicBridge → dashboard
Overview (pulsing halos, rotating intel strip, Crop Health Index).
SAY: "So we built EconomicBridge — a satellite-intelligence platform that
watches over farmland from space. It's live right now, monitoring over seven
hundred local government areas across Nigeria, Ghana and Senegal — streaming
real data from Copernicus satellites, NASA, the World Bank and UNICEF, every
single day."

## SEG 3 — It works (0:40–1:15) — three ~12s scenes
1) Farmland Protection: pulsing alert halos → click alert → 24–72h timeline.
2) CropGuard (money shot): real maize leaf held to camera (field), then
   screen rec: upload that leaf's photo → TRAINED badge → Grad-CAM diagnosis.
3) ShockGuard: flood/drought events on the map.
SAY: "Farmland Protection predicts farmer-herder conflict twenty-four to
seventy-two hours before it erupts. CropGuard — watch this — I photograph a
sick leaf, and our AI, trained on thousands of crop images, diagnoses the
disease in seconds and shows exactly where it looked. ShockGuard catches
floods and droughts from radar that sees through clouds."

## SEG 4 — Inclusion (1:15–1:35)
SHOW: Farmer/colleague with a BASIC feature phone showing an SMS (field);
Admin SMS panel with multilingual previews (EN/FR/HA visible).
SAY: "And here's what matters most: the farmer needs no smartphone, no app,
no registration — alerts reach them as simple SMS, in their own language,
through the agencies they already trust."

## SEG 5 — Credibility + vision (1:35–2:00)
SHOW: Overview again (system status bar), cut to founder at camera; end card:
wordmark + live URL + "Bizra Farms Integrated Nigeria Limited".
SAY: "Our team already built the security platform Kebbi State government
uses today. EconomicBridge is its next chapter — live on the cloud, with
state governments now joining as beta partners. We're designed for fifty-two
administrative units across West Africa. Help us make every farmer visible,
every harvest protected, and every naira of aid reach the right village.
EconomicBridge — planting the seeds of tomorrow."

## Recording patterns
VOICE: quiet room, phone ~30cm, 3 takes per segment, 10% slower than natural,
record 10s of room silence at the end (noise-cleanup reference).
FIELD: landscape 1080p/4K, sun behind camera, golden hour, hold each shot
8–10s perfectly still, 2–3 takes; shots: walking the field, hands+leaf
close-up, feature-phone SMS, founder to camera (open + close).
SCREEN: full-screen browser (F11), bookmarks hidden, 100–125% zoom,
super-admin login, slow deliberate mouse (pause 2s on readables), one clip
per module scene 20–30s; CropGuard upload→TRAINED→Grad-CAM in one take.
Extras/alternates: Mobility dual-currency, Reports PDF export, Scheduler
Recent Runs.
HAND-OFF: name files voice-segN / field-* / screen-*; give the editor this
script as the edit map; request light hopeful instrumental, SUBTITLES (judges
watch muted), end card with URL.
