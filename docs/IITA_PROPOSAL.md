## One-line pitch
Detect cassava mosaic & brown-streak outbreaks earlier, target them to the LGA, and push validated advice to farmers who have no smartphone — by pairing IITA's ground science with EconomicBridge's satellite platform and last-mile SMS.

## The opportunity
EconomicBridge runs a live, multi-tenant satellite-intelligence platform over West African administrative units (8 Nigerian states + Ghana + Senegal, full LGA resolution). Its **CropGuard** module is a trained crop-disease classifier whose classes include **cassava mosaic disease (CMD)** and **cassava brown-streak disease (CBSD)** — the exact diseases IITA leads the world on. The model is currently trained on open imagery (PlantVillage/Kaggle) and **needs field validation** — which is precisely where IITA's partnership creates value.

## What each partner brings
| EconomicBridge | IITA |
|---|---|
| CropGuard disease classifier | Validated field imagery + CMD/CBSD labels |
| Sentinel-2 NDVI anomaly surveillance (landscape stress) | Agronomy, diagnostics, field scouts |
| Per-LGA dashboards (multi-tenant, government-ready) | Seed-system & extension networks (BASICS) |
| Last-mile **SMS** advisory (no smartphone needed) | Research credibility + donor pipeline |

## The surveillance → diagnosis → advisory loop
1. **Satellite** flags where vegetation stress is rising (Sentinel-2 NDVI anomaly), per LGA.
2. **IITA / extension** ground-checks and confirms the disease.
3. **Platform** pushes a targeted **SMS advisory** (local language) to farmers in that LGA.

## Scoped pilot (6–9 months)
1. **Validate & retrain** CropGuard on IITA's labeled cassava field imagery.
2. **Stand up one cassava-belt state** (e.g. Benue) with the NDVI-anomaly surveillance layer.
3. **Run the field loop** above in 2–3 LGAs.
4. **Measure:** detection lead-time; diagnostic accuracy vs IITA ground truth; farmers reached by SMS; advisories acted on.

## Why it's mutually valuable
- **IITA** gains a deployment + dissemination layer (dashboards + SMS reach + government tenants) for research it already does, plus a co-authored digital-ag flagship.
- **EconomicBridge** gains field-validated labels (lifting CropGuard off lab accuracy) and scientific credibility.

## Complementary to Nuru (important framing)
IITA co-built **Nuru** (in-hand PlantVillage cassava AI). This is **not** a competing diagnosis app — it adds what Nuru doesn't: **landscape-scale satellite surveillance + per-LGA government dashboards + SMS to non-smartphone farmers + multi-tenant scale**. CropGuard's in-field model can feed IITA's validation pipeline.

## Adjacent angles (optional)
Multi-crop expansion (maize, plantain/banana, yam); aflatoxin targeting with **Aflasafe**; seed-system (BASICS) disease-pressure monitoring; **IITA Youth Agripreneurs** as the scouting + SMS-onboarding force.

## Funding
Co-apply rather than ask IITA to fund: CGIAR initiatives, **Gates Foundation** (major IITA cassava funder), GIZ, AfDB, BMGF AgTech. IITA brings the donor pipeline; EconomicBridge brings deployable tech.

A 30-minute call to scope a joint pilot and a shared data-sharing/validation arrangement, with a view to co-applying for [funder] in [timeframe].
